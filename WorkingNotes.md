## Drive Shopping
Advice to use microsd cards with adaptors.
They have ratings for speed that are reliable. 
Look for stuff with the A2 specification, its specifically for OS's 
Triple layer cells have better lifespan, if you can get that info.
32 gb might offer better pricing

Talk to URS about seeing if any of their suppliers have them and ask them to order them.
